About Me

I am an accounting graduate completing a BCom Honours in Taxation at the University of the Free State. I currently serve as an Academic Coordinator, where I support teaching teams, manage student performance data, and contribute to student engagement and development.

I work at the intersection of taxation, academic development, and technology. I enjoy building practical tools that make learning clearer, more accessible, and more relevant. As a committed lifelong learner, I strive to make education meaningful and appealing to students through innovative approaches and clear communication.
